💡 Pro Tips for Image Selection

  • Quality First: Use professional, high-resolution photographs
  • Consistency: Maintain similar lighting and style across all images
  • Aspect Ratios: Try to keep consistent ratios within each section
  • Compression: Always compress images before uploading (use TinyPNG or ImageOptim)
  • Format: JPG for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency
  • Naming: Use lowercase, hyphens instead of spaces (e.g., exterior-view.jpg)
  • Backup: Keep original high-resolution versions in a separate folder
